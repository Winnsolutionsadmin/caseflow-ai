## Ad Copy Testing Framework

### Elements to Test:
- **Headlines:** Pain vs benefit vs feature focused
- **CTAs:** "Learn More" vs "Get Demo" vs "Start Trial"
- **Social Proof:** Client names vs numbers vs testimonials
- **Urgency:** Limited spots vs time-sensitive offers
- **Benefit Focus:** Time savings vs revenue vs risk reduction

### Testing Methodology:
- Run 2-3 ad variations simultaneously
- Minimum 100 clicks per variation for statistical significance
- Test one element at a time
- 2-week minimum test duration
- Winner becomes control for next test
