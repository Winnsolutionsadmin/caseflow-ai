## Subject Line Optimization Guidelines

### High-Performance Patterns:
- **Specific numbers:** "The $47K mistake" vs "Expensive mistake"
- **Direct questions:** "Losing 12 billable hours per week?"
- **Firm name personalization:** "[Firm Name] revenue leakage analysis"
- **Deadline urgency:** "Missed deadline in Sacramento County?"
- **Social proof:** "[Similar Firm] case study"

### Avoid These Patterns:
- Generic legal software language ("solutions," "streamline")
- All caps or excessive punctuation
- Overly salesy ("REVOLUTIONARY," "GAME-CHANGING")
- Generic greetings ("Hope you're well")
- Vague benefits ("improve efficiency")
